About Me

Frontend Developer & Designer

I'm a passionate full-stack developer with a strong foundation in computer science, backed by a BSc degree in Computer Science. From an early stage in my academic journey, I was deeply interested in how technology works behind the scenes—from the logic that drives applications to the interfaces that connect users to powerful systems. Whether working on solo projects or as part of a team, I always strive to deliver scalable, efficient, and impactful solutions.
